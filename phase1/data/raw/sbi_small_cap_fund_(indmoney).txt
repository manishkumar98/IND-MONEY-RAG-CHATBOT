About SBI Small Cap Fund

The SBI Small Cap Fund is an equity mutual fund from SBI Mutual Fund that invests primarily in a diversified portfolio of small-cap company stocks for long-term growth. It uses a bottom-up strategy to select stocks and can invest up to 35% of its assets in other equities (including large and mid-cap) or debt instruments.

The fund is benchmarked against the BSE 250 SmallCap Total Return Index. Currently, the Assets Under Management (AUM) for the fund is approximately ₹36,945 Crore.
